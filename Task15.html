FAQ

What is Tata Play Fiber?

Tata Play Fiber is a broadband internet service provided by Tata Play.

How do I get a new connection?

You can visit the Tata Play website or contact their customer care to get a new connection.

What is included in the router plans?

The router plans include a Wi-Fi router and internet access. Specific features vary by plan.

What is the installation process like?

A technician will visit your premises to install the router and set up the connection. They will also guide you through the basic usage.

What if I face any issues with my connection?

You can contact Tata Play customer support through phone, email, or their website. They will assist you in resolving any issues you may face.
